A simple multiplayer game where you can challenge your friends for fast and fun games!

Download App

'Find or Fall' is a casual, multiplayer video game for Android devices, where two persons must guess where is their opponent's button that makes him fall, they have eight buttons, were the ones that open the opponent's hatches will be hidden but also the one that opens their own.

How the idea of ​​"find or fall" came about ?

The idea arose from the intention of understanding technologies that none of the members used, by implementing an online and multiplayer gameplay which is the differentiator of this game, in addition to the simplicity and fast entertainment. The game was intended to be visually entertaining with easy to understand mechanics, to have funny animations and only luck was the ability to win each game. Of course, the fact of finishing a project and carrying out the entire launch was something that encouraged each member to be part of this project and participate in the idea since each one of them has, within their activities, enjoy video games.
